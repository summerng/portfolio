A Basic Introduction to Sequential Art & Comics

Hi everyone! I’m Summer, and I am a auto-biographical comic artist. I love what I do, and I’m glad I get to share my work with the world. It gives me a platform to share my open, honest thoughts in a way that feels unique to myself.

However...

You might be thinking, “comics? As in superhero comic books?”

To which I say, “No -- the field of comic art is much broader than it seems!” This website contains information on the history of comics, examples of amazing comic artists, and the possibilities of comic art. Comic art is definitely an art - it involves drawing, writing, problem solving, and creativity. I hope to provide a peek into the world of comics, so that you can start reading or making your own!
